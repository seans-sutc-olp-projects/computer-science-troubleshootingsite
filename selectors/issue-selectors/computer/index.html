Common Issues

Computer will not turn on:
  • check the surge protector or no-break,
  • check the power outlets,
  • check the power cord.
Computer turns on, but no image appears on screen:

check the monitor cable or VGA cable.

Computer restarting on its own:
  • check the internal temperature of the enclosure,
  • scan for viruses,
  • spyware among others,
  • check for dirt on contacts,
  • check the electrical installations.
Computer turns on, but the screen goes black and beeps: warningcheck if the video card and other cards are firmly placed in the slots.
The operating system clock is slow: warningcheck the coin battery and replace it.
The computer is slow or crashes:
  • check if the processor is heating up,
  • scan for viruses,
  • spyware among others,
  • warningcheck if the logic boards are firmly secured,
  • check if the hard drive is too full,
  • check if the power grid has problems.
The surge protector or no-break does not turn on:
  • check for power problems,
  • warningcheck fuse or internal capacitor.
